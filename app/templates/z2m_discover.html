Once your Zigbee dongle arrives, you'll need two services running:

1. Mosquitto (MQTT broker)

brew install mosquitto
brew services start mosquitto

2. Zigbee2MQTT

npm install -g zigbee2mqtt
# Edit ~/.config/zigbee2mqtt/configuration.yaml — set your adapter port
zigbee2mqtt

Then come back here to discover your devices.
